Tiramisu

Description

A classic Italian dessert made of coffee-soaked lady fingers, creamy marscapone, and dusted cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 8 ounce marscapone cheese, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups cold espresso
  • 3 Tablespoons coffe flavoured liquer
  • 1 package Lady Fingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting on top
